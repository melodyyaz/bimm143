All fuction in R have 3 things: 1. name, in our case “grade” 2.input arguments, in our case the student’s grade vector 3. body, this is our working snippet above

grade <- function(x)
{
#replace NA values with 0
x[is.na(x)] <- 0
#Exclude the lowest score & calculate mean
mean(x[-which.min(x)])
}
